    The best way to throw yourself into the Zoo experience is to join our family. As Zoo members, you and your own family will enjoy year round access to more than 4,000 animals and 710 acres of adventure. Not to mention the thrill of helping us protect wildlife. How easy is that?



    All new membership and parking pass purchases are effective from the date of purchase. Memberships do not require shipping.



    Member benefits

    • Unlimited free admission to the Zoo for duration of membership.
    • Free Zoomobile rides (Seasonal)
    • Program and Zoo Camp discount.
    • 10% discount on purchases in our Giftshops.
    • 10% discount on purchases in Restaurants (excluding remote carts).
    • Receive invitations to special events and exhibit previews.
    • Admission discount to AZA accredited zoo and aquariums in North America.
